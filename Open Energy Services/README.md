# WeForming Open Energy Services

### Enabling Intelligent Grid-Forming Buildings in Open Energy Ecosystems

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Overview

**WeForming Open Energy Services** provide a modular, interoperable, and
scalable digital service ecosystem that enables buildings to operate as
**Intelligent Grid-Forming Buildings (iGFBs)** within local and regional
energy systems.

Rather than functioning as passive energy consumers, buildings become:

-   Active grid participants\
-   Flexibility providers\
-   Market-aware energy actors\
-   Semantically interoperable digital entities

These services are built upon the WeForming digital architecture,
including:

-   Canonical Dataspace Model (CDM)\
-   W-IBRA API Gateway\
-   Semantic Digital Twins\
-   AI/ML forecasting and optimisation services\
-   Secure Dataspace IAM integration

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Vision

WeForming Open Energy Services aim to:

-   Enable **building-to-grid interaction**
-   Facilitate **energy flexibility markets participation**
-   Support **real-time monitoring and control**
-   Provide **semantic interoperability across heterogeneous platforms**
-   Extend **Smart Readiness Indicator (SRI)** capabilities into dynamic
    digital services

The long-term goal is a federated, semantically aligned, AI-enhanced
ecosystem where buildings and grids operate as coordinated energy
communities.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Architectural Foundation

### 3.1 W-IBRA Interoperability Architecture

At the core of Open Energy Services lies the **WeForming Interoperable
Building-Grid Architecture (W-IBRA)**, composed of:

1.  **W-IBRA Connector**
    -   Dataspace communication layer\
    -   Secure data exchange\
    -   GAIA-X / EDC protocol compatibility
2.  **API Gateway (Demo-Specific)**
    -   Canonical data transformation\
    -   Schema validation\
    -   Service routing\
    -   IAM integration
3.  **Canonical Dataspace Model (CDM)**
    -   JSON-LD envelope structure\
    -   Schema-driven validation\
    -   Semantic harmonisation\
    -   Governance & traceability metadata
4.  **Backend Services & Digital Twins**
    -   Simulation engines\
    -   Forecasting models\
    -   Market participation tools\
    -   Energy management systems
